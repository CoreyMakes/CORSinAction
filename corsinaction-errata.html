CORS in Action: Errata

Hi there! Thank you for taking the time to read CORS in Action. This errata lists any errors in the book discovered after publication. If you find any errors, please report them on the Author Online Forum. I will continue to update this page as new issues are found. Thanks!

  • Chapter 2, Listing 2.1

The labels for "Sends request" and "Processes response" should be switched. xhr.onload should be labeled as "Processes response", while xhr.send() should be labeled as "Sends request".
